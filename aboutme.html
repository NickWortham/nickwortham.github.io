$andman is the stage name of Brian Desautels, a 29 year old musician and artist from the Monterey Bay area. He is known for his unpredictable, eclectic live DJ sets which can last for hours and frequently span multiple genres of dance music. Experience playing a multitude of instruments and knowledge of music theory help distinguish his performances from the average noise, noting subtle distinctions in harmonics and key which highlight the theatricality of the mix. Typical encounters leave participants awash in feelings of empathy and love as bass vibrations overwhelm all senses!
